Customer Diaries

Here at Chandan Fashion, we strive to give our customers an exciting and extraordinary experience when deciding upon the most important dress for their lifetime occasion. We make every attempt to give you personal attention with our professional help at every step. With a rich experience of more than 3 decades in bridal wear, we have catered to diverse set of customers from different cultures and nationalities and build trust in providing them with true ethnic designs and styles for their weddings and family gatherings.

We emphasize on building relationships with our customers through understanding their needs, undivided attention and solving their queries on custom order process to make it a seamless and an unparalleled experience in their journey of finding the perfect outfit for their special occasions.

We feel proud to say that our team collectively contributes their efforts to assist customers with the relevant information on our bridal as well as party wear collections and ensure complete customer satisfaction. We offer custom made bridal dresses where customers get a lot of choices as well as flexibility in terms of fabric, embroidery, styles and colours bringing authentic taste and modernity to the dresses.

Our dresses and wedding gowns demonstrate a kind of love, simplicity and uniqueness. Our bespoke bridal outfits portray the rich culture and ethnicity of the tradition and values of South Asian weddings. Every outfit at our store goes through a journey, from sketches to embroidery swatches and then to the custom fit wedding attire. Our dresses carry an art of storytelling and our brides and grooms bring those stories to life when they make them a part of their auspicious and most important day.
